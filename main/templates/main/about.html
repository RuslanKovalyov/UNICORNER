UNICORNER COFFEE

Where Great Coffee Meets Bright Minds

Welcome to UNICORNER COFFEE, a one-of-a-kind café where top-tier coffee meets the innovative spirit of Tel Aviv’s tech community. Located at Sderot Yehudit 29, we aim to create a cozy, welcoming space for developers, entrepreneurs, and coffee enthusiasts to connect, collaborate, and recharge.

Our Concept

  • Unicorn: A nod to the ambition and innovation of startups and groundbreaking ideas.
  • Corner: A meeting spot where creativity and collaboration flourish.
  • Unified: Bringing people together over a shared love for coffee and technology.

What We Offer

Whether you're stopping by for a cup of our hand-crafted coffee or joining our community of tech enthusiasts, UNICORNER COFFEE is more than a café—it's an experience. Here’s what we’re all about:

  • Freshly roasted coffee beans and artisan beverages.
  • A tech-infused atmosphere for inspiration and networking.
  • Exclusive access to community events, hackathons, and pitch nights.
  • Open-source projects for collaborative growth and innovation.

Our Vision

At UNICORNER COFFEE, we’re not just serving coffee—we’re building a movement. From our Tel Aviv branch, we envision expanding globally, creating vibrant spaces in major tech hubs like Silicon Valley, Berlin, and more. Our mission is to combine coffee culture with the collaborative spirit of technology.

Join the UNICORNER Community

Become a part of our journey by visiting our café, contributing to our open-source projects, or sharing your ideas. At UNICORNER COFFEE, every cup of coffee brings people and possibilities together.
